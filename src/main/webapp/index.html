Reception hall

Reception hall serves as the gateway to medical care, providing a welcoming space for patients, visitors, and staff. It facilitates check-ins, inquiries, and ensures a smooth entry into healthcare services.

General Ward

The general ward is a fundamental healthcare unit providing essential medical care to patients. It accommodates individuals with various health conditions, offering a standard level of care and monitoring.

03

Cardiology Ward

Cardiology is the specialized branch of medicine focused on the heart and circulatory system. Cardiologists diagnose, treat, and prevent cardiovascular diseases, ensuring optimal heart health through expertise in advanced medical interventions.

04

Gynecology

The gynecology ward is a specialized healthcare unit catering to women's reproductive health. Staffed by skilled professionals, it provides comprehensive care for gynecological issues, maternity care, and related medical services.

05

Orthopedic Ward

The orthopedic ward specializes in musculoskeletal health, treating conditions related to bones, joints, and muscles. With a focus on orthopedic surgery and rehabilitation, it offers comprehensive care for patients recovering from orthopedic issues.

06

ICU

The ICU (Intensive Care Unit) is a critical medical facility where patients with severe illnesses or life-threatening conditions receive specialized and continuous care. Skilled healthcare professionals monitor and administer advanced treatments for optimal recovery.
